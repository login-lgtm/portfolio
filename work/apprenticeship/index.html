Process
How I approached it.
  • 01 Treated the apprenticeship as a starting point — took on real client work alongside it from early on
  • 02 Built across formats: print, web, corporate identity, editorial — often without a template to follow
  • 03 Made decisions independently — even when the brief was unclear or the brief didn't exist yet
  • 04 Finished with a broader skillset than the programme required — and a clearer direction for what comes next
What I learned

"Real work taught me what no classroom could — how to make decisions, take responsibility and keep learning while things are moving."

Building while learning made me resourceful, independent and comfortable with not knowing everything yet.
