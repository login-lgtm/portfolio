mi4l — project info
From feeling to identity.

Co-founded MI4L in December 2022 alongside my partner Leon. Supported the creative direction from day one — contributing ideas, visuals and brand thinking. Took on full Creative Lead responsibility in 2024 during the final year of my apprenticeship.

The visual systems existed before I stepped fully in — I developed, refined and scaled them. The brand grew as I grew.
