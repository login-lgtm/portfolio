mi4l — project info
From an idea to a brand.

MI4L started as a small event series founded by friends who wanted to create the kind of events they couldn't find themselves.

I was involved from the beginning, helping build the brand while learning through real projects, real events and real audiences.

Over time, the project evolved into multiple formats, collaborations and a distinct visual identity — a space for community, experimentation and creative growth.

In 2025, I stepped into the role of Creative Lead, taking on full responsibility for communication, campaigns and the ongoing evolution of the brand.

Process
How I approached it.
  • 01 Built from scratch — no brief, no agency, no budget. Defined the visual direction together with the founders
  • 02 Developed a visual system that works across poster, digital, merch and on-site — not just for one event
  • 03 Applied and refined the system across 15+ real events over three years
  • 04 Adapted the identity as the event grew — without losing what made it recognisable from the start
What I learned

"Brand consistency is what turns an event into an identity."

Designing across formats taught me to think in systems, not single assets.
